ABOUT HOME TUTORIAL

Home Tutorial basically is the avenue where programming courses are provided and taught to students from the comfort of their homes (only available for residents of Abuja).

The goal of this type of tutoring is to help students overcome academic challenges and lead them to an autonomous/independent learning through exclusive teaching with one-to-one attention.

Through home tutoring, Parents and home tutor's are better able to assess the growth, performance, and area(s) where the student lags behind.
